Classic Marshmallow fruit dip

Description

We love nothing more than snacking on fresh fruit in the summer. Everything is perfectly in season, and just tastes so deliciously sweet. Sometimes weâ€™re looking for that little extra kick of something special to go with plain fruit, which is where marshmallow fruit dip comes in. Not only does it taste amazing, it also makes your snack of fresh fruit a bit more filling to get you through the day.

Ingredients

  • 7 oz marshamllow creme
  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, use a hand mixer to beat together marshmallow creme, cream cheese, and vanilla extract until well combined and creamy, about 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. Serve immediately with your favorite fruits or store covered, in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.
  3. Popular fruits for dipping include strawberries, grapes, pineapple, blueberries, kiwis, bananas, raspberries, mandarin slices, cantaloupe, and apples.
