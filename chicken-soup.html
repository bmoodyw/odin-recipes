Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 medium boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons salted butter
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup sliced celery
  • 1 medium white onion diced

Instructions

  1. In a large 6 quart pot, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear in pot for 3 to 4 minutes per side, until seared and browned. Remove from pot and set aside on a plate.
  3. Without wiping out the pan, add in butter onion, carrots, and celery and saute until vegetables just start to become tender, about 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. Add ginger and garlic and saute for 2 minutes more. Add chicken breasts back on top of vegetables.
